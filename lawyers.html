The problem

A lost USB is a confidentiality breach waiting to happen.

Case files and evidence routinely leave your office on physical media. A plain, unencrypted USB drive means that if it's ever lost, left behind, or handed to the wrong person, everything on it is instantly readable — client confidentiality, attorney-client privilege, and your firm's reputation along with it.

How FileLocker helps

Encrypted delivery your clients can actually use.

FileLocker encrypts the file with AES-256-GCM before it ever leaves your office. Your client opens a single HTML file in their browser and unlocks it with a password you've shared separately — no software install, no account, no confusion for a non-technical client.
