Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What data we collect

FileLocker the desktop application operates entirely offline. It does not collect, transmit, or store any of your files, passwords, or usage data on any server — there is no server for it to send data to. The only data we collect is limited to what you provide when visiting this website or contacting us: your name and email address if you fill out the contact form, and standard, anonymized web analytics (page views, general location by country, browser type) if analytics are enabled on this site.

How we use it

Contact form submissions are used solely to respond to your enquiry. Analytics data, if collected, is used in aggregate to understand which pages are useful and where visitors run into friction — never to identify you individually.

Cookie policy

This website may use minimal, essential cookies for basic site functionality and anonymized analytics. We do not use cookies for cross-site advertising or third-party tracking.

Contact form data

Information submitted through the contact form is retained only as long as necessary to resolve your enquiry, and is not shared with third parties beyond what's needed to respond to you (for example, an email delivery provider).

Third-party services

We may use third-party services for website hosting, email delivery, and analytics. These providers only receive the minimum data necessary to perform their function and are not authorized to use it for any other purpose.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under GDPR or similar regulations to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Contact for privacy concerns

Reach out via the Contact page with any privacy-related questions or requests.
