Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

At FileLocker, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the website FileLocker (the "Website") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

2. Information We Collect

FileLocker is designed with privacy in mind. Our desktop application operates 100% offline and does not transmit any of your encrypted files, passwords, or keys to our servers. We only collect basic account information (such as your email address) when you purchase a license or log in to the Website.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are securely processed by our payment partner, Lemon Squeezy. We do not store or process any credit card information on our servers. Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy governs the collection and processing of your payment details.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to manage your software license keys, provide customer support, and notify you about major product updates. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@filelocker.com.
